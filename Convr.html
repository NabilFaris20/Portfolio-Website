Core Concept

Construction VR is a VR simulation game that aims to recreate the process of building a house in a fun and engaging manner. It also aims to promote the use of sustainable materials in construction to raise awareness and prevent further complications towards the planet such as global warming due to the non-sustainable materials used in construction.
