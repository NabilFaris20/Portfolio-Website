My Work

My Mechanical Mother

My Mechanical Mother is a story-driven VR game based on the experiences of a young genius boy who has recently lost his mother. In an attempt to recreate the love and care that his mother used to give him, he creates a robot that mimics the actions of his mother and tries to find happiness in the likeness.

Construction VR

Construction VR is a VR simulator type game that involves the construction of a house. Players will construct the house from ground up, putting up the structural beams, wall panels, roofing, etc.
Construction VR isn't just a simulator however, it also promotes the use of sustainable materials in construction to raise awareness and prevent further complications towards the planet such as global warming.

The Big Step

The Big Step is about a 15 year old boy named Jamil who is trying to save his friend from the clutches of obesity. Jamil's friend Siti comes back to the kampung from the city for a vacation, but appears to have become morbidly obese. In an attempt to help his friend lose weight, Jamil goes on a journey to find various items which will help Siti to lose weight.
Players will be playing as Jamil, and will journey through various environments and locations while needing to solve puzzles and mini-games that are of varying difficulty in order to attain the items needed to help Siti lose weight.
